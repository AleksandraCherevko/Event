Services

Services and features

CBD plans events in Washington, DC, nationally in Chicago, Atlanta, Miami, Los Angeles, Las Vegas and New York, in the Bahamas and for our international partners.

Our ready approach to spectacular events is second to none. We employ graphic designers, logistics managers, public relations specialists and have our own offset and digital printing component. We have floor plans and specifications for nearly every location, convention center, meeting space in Washington, DC, and excellent relationships with our third-party vendors. We also offer Spanish, Portuguese, French and German translation services.

What can we do?

We can show you better than tell.

Service selection

  • Reminder list for guests

    Possibilities include guest list management through our online corporate client website or through the pre-event CBD registration system, ensuring your guests are accounted for.

  • Event planning and production

    This is a serious process. Our trained event planners cover every detail. Capabilities include: concept design, decor and venue negotiations.

  • Event decor and floral design

    Floral design, production of props, lighting, special bedding and much more are delicately selected by the employees of our design team.

  • Marketing and public relations

    All aspects of marketing and public relations are handled by the Communications Agency. We offer turnkey solutions for branding, product advertising and messaging.

  • Concierge and protocol

    CBD hosts events in some of the safest places in Washington, DC. We are experts in obtaining event permits, security clearances, guest arrival protocol and negotiation standards.

  • Sound and audiovisual

    Sound and audiovisual requirements for filmed concerts and speakers' performances, small and large meetings. We support: PowerPoint presentations, webinars, Skype, digital and social media.

  • Guest registration

    The first impression can cause excitement or spoil the mood of the guest. The CBD registration process has been carefully designed to ensure an easy registration process.

  • Lighting for special events

    Special event lighting options include overhead lighting, custom lighting, illuminated event furniture, filming lighting, and other specialty lighting.

  • Graphic design and invitations

    We design formal invitations: letterpress, cotton, linen and custom services such as calligraphy. We also sell handmade invitation cards.
