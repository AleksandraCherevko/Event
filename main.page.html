About company

CBD Meetings & Events is Washington, DC's turnkey solution to complete event planning, event management and event marketing. CBD is the best integrated meeting planner for the international community. We proudly serve 8 countries on 5 continents. CBD manages events for diplomats, non-profit organizations, foundations and associations, corporations and individuals.
