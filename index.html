About CBD: Event Planning

The best organizer in planning and conducting various events

  • International meetings
  • Specialty: Foreign private sector investment seminars

Event planning organization headquartered in Washington, DC. CBD manages events for international governments, non-profit organizations, corporations, small businesses and individuals. We are a small business. Our owner is a woman who holds memberships in Washington Women In PR and Destination DC.

Our corporate department offers conference planning, annual meetings and event management. Our social department plans social events, non-profit fundraisers and weddings.

We have managed events from conceptualization to completion for several historic and highly publicized events such as: The Journer Truth Unveiling with First Lady of the United States Ms. Michelle Obama, US Secretary Hillary Clinton, Government of Brazil, Embassy of Sweden, Coca-Cola, Time Warner, Dodge, Atlanta Committee of the Olympic Games (ACOG) and many others.

Turnkey solutions: Events we manage

We are planning events in Washington, DC, nationally, in the Bahamas, and for our international partners.

Our turnkey approach to spectacular events is unmatched. We are hiring Graphic Designers, Logistics Managers, Public Relations Specialists and have our own offset and digital printing component.

We have floor plans and specifications for nearly every facility, convention center, and meeting space in the Washington, DC metropolitan area and excellent relationships with our third-party vendors. We also offer translation services in Spanish, Portuguese, French and German.
